Nacho Burrito

Have you ever had nachos and been disappointed by the little bits left on the plate at the end? This is the solution for you!

Ingredients:

  • Cheese sauce
  • Nachos
  • Tortilla

Directions:

  1. Place your tortilla on your plate/in your bowl/wherever you plan to serve your food.
  2. Make your nachos how you like and place them directly on the tortilla.
  3. Fill up a shot glass with your extra cheese sauce for a little fun.
  4. Enjoy your nachos as usual and roll the tortilla into a burrito to finish the leftover bits of nacho.
