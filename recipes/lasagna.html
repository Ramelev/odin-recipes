Lasagna

A traditional* lasagna, with half the time and effort, and none of the sauce! *(image not representative of final product)

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound bulk italian sausage
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1 pinch dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 8 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 3 pounds mozzarella cheese, shredded

Directions:

  1. Sear off the ground beef and italian sausage in a pan or dutch oven. Remove from the pan.
  2. Pour the warm water into the pan and add your spices, turn the heat to medium, and place the noodles in the water for 5 minutes.
  3. Add both cheeses to your spiced water and continue cooking for another 5 minutes.
  4. Once both of the cheeses are stringy and inedible, add your meats back into the pan and cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Remove from the stove and place directly into an oven set to 500Â°F, and cook for 25 minutes.
  6. Serve immediately with a side of burnt french bread.
